# 📊 Amazon Sales Power BI Dashboard

This Power BI dashboard provides an interactive analysis of Amazon sales data. It offers a comprehensive view of key metrics like product categories, sales performance, geographical sales distribution, and more.

# 🗂️ Dashboard Features

### Product Category
Analyze sales performance by different product categories.

### Sales
View total sales figures and detailed breakdowns.

### Sales by City & State
Geographical visualization of sales across various cities and states.

### Total Sales
Summarize overall revenue from all products and regions.

### Seller Count
Track the number of active sellers contributing to sales.

### Units Sold by Date
Observe sales trends over time with a date-wise breakdown.

### Loss & Sale Amount
Evaluate financial performance by tracking both loss and total sale amount.

# 📋 Pages Overview

### Overview
Provides a high-level summary of key sales metrics and trends.

### Products
Detailed analysis of product performance, including category-wise breakdowns.

### Product View
In-depth visualization of individual product details and performance.
